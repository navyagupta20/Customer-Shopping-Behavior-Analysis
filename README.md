# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## **📌 Overview**
Analyzed **3,900 customer transactions** to uncover shopping patterns, customer segments, subscription trends, and product performance. The project combines **Python, SQL and Power BI** to generate actionable business insights.

---

## **🛠️ Tools Used**

Python  
SQL  
Power BI  

---

## **📊 Key Analysis**
- Revenue by Gender & Age Group
- Customer Segmentation
- Subscriber vs Non-Subscriber Analysis
- Top-Rated Products
- Discount Impact Analysis
- Shipping Preference Analysis

---

## **📈 Dashboard Highlights**
✔ Revenue Overview  
✔ Customer Segmentation  
✔ Product Performance  
✔ Subscription Insights  
✔ Interactive Filters & KPIs  

---

## **💡 Key Insights**
- Subscribers contribute higher revenue.
- Loyal customers drive significant sales.
- Some products are highly discount-dependent.
- Revenue varies across customer age groups.

---

## **🚀 Business Recommendations**
- Promote subscription benefits.
- Introduce customer loyalty programs.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Focus marketing on high-value customer segments.
